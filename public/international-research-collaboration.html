International Collaboration & Expertise

Academic & Training Background

During his academic career and training at Institute in Advanced orthopedics in Munich Germany, Dr Mir Jawad Zar Khan had the opportunity to work with world renowned International orthopedic surgeons and gained vast experience in Joint replacements and Arthroscopy surgeries and Spine Surgeries. Dr Mir Jawad Zar Khan has performed more than 10,000 Joint replacements Arthroscopic and Spine Surgeries & vast number of complex Trauma cases over a period of 22 years at Germany and in India, and also experienced in non operative modes of treatments.

Research Foundation Collaboration

Dr Mir Jawad Zar Khan and his International doctors Team are collaborating an Orthopedic Research Foundation with all the International doctors in the Panel dedicated to stimulation of Orthopedic and musculoskeletal Research in fields of Arthroscopy, Traumatology and Joint Surgeries and Spine surgeries. The principal aim of this Research is to create long-term, sustainable solutions to the growing burden of musculoskeletal injuries and disorders through academic partnership. Mainly focusing on both operative & Non operative techniques in Orthopedics and neurology. This collaboration seeks to facilitate communication with member organisations to enhance international research collaborations and to promote the development of new international orthopedic and musculoskeletal research organisations. All the associate Doctors have more than two decades of teaching and research expertise in German University Hospital apart from performing thousands of orthopedic and Spine Surgeries.
