Our Specialities

Our Orthopaedic Specialty Centre offers its overseas patients world-class treatment, personalized attention, and a comfortable stay.

Patients can email us about their medical condition, along with the investigation reports. Based on the transaction, we can arrive at a likely diagnosis and treatment and suggest a travel itinerary. A fully furnished Guest House near our hospital is available for patients and attendants. If needed, local transportation can also be arranged by our travel desk.

Overseas patients are assisted by our Public Relations Executive for doctor's consults, lab, radiology reports, follow-ups and appointments, to enable hassle-free procedures.

OVERSEAS PATIENTS AT OUR HOSPITAL

We frequently provide treatment for patients from Middle East and South East Asian countries including NRIs.

The number of overseas patients being treated at our hospital has risen steadily due to the following reasons.

  • Top notch Health Care System and Practices
  • German based latest precision technology for joint replacement, Sports Injuries, Spine Surgeries
  • Reliable medical reports
  • Proper maintenance and confidentiality of patient records
  • Clean and serene atmosphere
  • NABH accreditation international standards
  • Competitive rates

Recently, we have had patients from many countries like Maldives, Sri Lanka, UAE, Oman, USA, United Kingdom, Germany, Malaysia, Bahrain, Qatar, South Africa, Nigeria, France, Ghana, Bangladesh, Kuwait, Australia, Singapore, and Dubai.
